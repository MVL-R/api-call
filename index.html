Welcome to the Monster Hunter World

In the world of MH, you belong to the Guild, an organization dedicated to the equilibrium between humans and the planet. Their main objective is to minimize the damage to the ecosystem and avoid the extinction of monsters and such. In this entry to the series, you will be visiting the New World. Though its existence was known for a long time, the New World remained unexplored because of how far away it was and because of the dangerous weather surrounding it. With the mysterious phenomenon of Elder Dragons migrating to the New World (called the Elder Crossing) and advances in seafaring technology, the Commission (a detachment of elite members of the Guild, including hunters, scholars, and engineers, all of whom were required to pass rigorous tests before they could join) was able to land five fleets on the New World.
